My Top 5 Tourist Attractions

The Big Ben

Big Ben is a famous clock tower located in London, England. It is one of the most recognizable landmarks in the city and a popular tourist attraction . The clock tower's chimes and distinctive appearance make it an iconic symbol of London's rich history and culture.

The Pyramids of Giza

The Pyramids of Giza are one of the most famous and impressive ancient wonders of the world. Located in Giza, Egypt, these pyramids were built over 4,500 years ago as tombs for pharaohs and their consorts.

Christ the Redeemer

Christ the Redeemer is a famous statue located in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil. Standing at 98 feet tall and weighing over 635 metric tons, this iconic statue of Jesus Christ is one of the largest and most impressive in the world.

Eiffel Tower

The Eiffel Tower is an iconic landmark located in Paris, France. Built in 1889 for the World's Fair, the tower stands at 1,063 feet tall and was the tallest man-made structure in the world until 1930.

Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal is an Islamic ivory-white marble mausoleum on the right bank of the river Yamuna in the Indian city of Agra
